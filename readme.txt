A 2-player game played on same computer.
Game Rules:
1)Each player can moved only horizontally.
2)Each player has 10 bouncing missile i.e. the missile after projecting bounces back after reaching the other side and can hit
the player who had shot it.
3)Each player gets a fair chancce to projct missile therefore one cannot project second missile unless the other player has 
projected its missile.
4)Any player who got hit by any missile(missile shot by both player) loses.
5)Game objective is to dodge missile until other player gets hit.
6)Game starts only when first player shoot its missile from same place as it is place.
Game controls:  
1st player - left and right arrow keys for movement and up arrow key for shooting
2nd player - 'A' and 'D' keys for movement and 'W' for shooting
